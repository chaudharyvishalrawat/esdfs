Indian History

Indus Valley Civilization

The Indus Valley Civilization was one of the world's oldest urban cultures, thriving around 2600-1900 BCE in what is now Pakistan and northwest India.

Maurya and Gupta Empires

The Maurya Empire (322–185 BCE) and Gupta Empire (320–550 CE) were significant periods in ancient Indian history, witnessing advancements in art, science, and philosophy.

Mughal Empire

The Mughal Empire (1526–1857) was a powerful empire in the Indian subcontinent known for its cultural contributions, including art, architecture, and literature.
